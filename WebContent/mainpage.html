Welcome to the Online Charity System

We connect donors with NGOs to make the world a better place.

About Us

Our platform provides a seamless way for donors to find and support NGOs that align with their values. Whether you want to donate money, goods, or your time, we make it easy to give back to the community.

How It Works

Donors can browse through various NGO projects, select the ones they wish to support, and make their contributions directly through our platform. NGOs can list their projects, provide updates, and manage donations efficiently.
